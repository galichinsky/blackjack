Welcome to Blackjack!

The objective of Blackjack is to beat the dealer by getting a hand value closer to 21 without going over.

  • Click on a the chips to make a bet.
  • Click "Deal" to draw the cards
  • Choose "Hit" to draw another card, or "Stand" to keep your current hand.
  • Try to get as close to 21 as possible without busting!
  • Gambling is a way of buying hope on credit - Please gamble responsably!
